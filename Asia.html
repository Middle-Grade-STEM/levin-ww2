Beligerants

This Website will try its best to try to summarize WW2 in Asia in easy and comprehensible way to everyone.
The main beligerants of the war were the Axis Powers and Allied Powers.
The main Axis Power's members consisted of Japan.
The main Allied Power's members consisted of USA, UK, France, China and Neatherlands.

Lead up to the War/War Begins

In the 1800s Japan quickly modernized and this lead them to look for more land.
Eventually this lead to them fighting China throughout the 1800s to the begining of the 1900s.
In the 1920s Japan attacked China again hoping to take more land but USA put a trade embargo on Japan.
In order to keep on expanding, Japan decided to attack the US navy so they can expand into the Pacific.
They decided to destroy the US navy by launching a surprise attack on Pearl Harbor in December 1941.
Due to this attack USA declared war on Japan and Japan proceeded to take the Philipines and other US islands.
Japan continued to expand by also taking colonies in Asia away from France, UK, and the Neatherlands.

War Ends

The turning point of the war in Asia began when the US navy heavily damaged the japanese navy at the Battle of Midway
The US then began an island hopping campain to retake isalnds and get closer to mainland Japan to bomb/invade.
The US also had devloped an atom bomb in 1945 and by then, they could send a plane to nuke mainland Japan.
To avoid the american death that would happen if they invaded mainland Japan, President Truman decided to drop the atom bomb on Japan.
After droping an atom bomb on Hiroshima and Nagasaki, Japan surrenedered ending the war in Asia and WW2.
