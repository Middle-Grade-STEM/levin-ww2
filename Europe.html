Beligerants

The main beligerants of the war were the Axis Powers and Allied Powers.

The main Axis Power's members consisted of Germany, Italy, and Romainia.

The main Allied Power's members consisted of USA, USSR, UK, France, Poland, Norway, Greece, Belgium, and Neatherlands.

Lead up to the War

When Germany was defeated in 1914, the allies gave Germany an unequal treaty leaving the germans angry.
Also in Italy, there was a fascist dictator rose to power and he wanted to expand Italy's land.
In Germany, a man called Adolf Hitler rose to power after years of failing democrocy in Germany and also became a fascist dictator.
Once Hitler rose to power he began to re-arm the military to take land for the germans and get revenge against the allies.

War Begins

After annexing some small countries around them, Germany made an non-agression pact with USSR in order to not fight a 2 front war, trade deals, and split up Poland
In September 1939 Germany invaded Poland which lead Great Britain and France to declare war on Germany. Despite UK and France declaring war, they barely attacked Germany.
This lead Poland to fight alone but after USSR invaded Poland, Poland fell and Germany began to focus on the other allies.
Not wanting to fight Germany on France's soil, the allies decided to blockade Germany to prevent them getting iron from Sweden. This lead to Germany invading Norway to prevent this
In order to get to Norway, Germany took over Denmark who surrenedered in hours, and sucesfully took over Norway. Finally Germany decided to focus on defeathing France.
In order to get around France's Forts, Germany invaded Belgium and Neatherlands, France and UK went to their defense but Germany outmanuvered them and surrounded their armies.
Because of this, UK retreated from France and France soon fell to the Germans, along with Belgium and Neatherlands. This left Uk as the only real threat against Germany for now.
Germany attempted to invade Britain but failed and decided that they could deal with UK later, so they began to focus on USSR.
Meanwhile Italy attempted to invade Greece but need help from Germany to defeat them, so Germany annexed Romainia and Yugoslavia to get there.
