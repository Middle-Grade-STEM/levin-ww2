War in Africa

In 1935 Italy wanted to build an empire so it began by invading Ethiopia, an indepedent african country and annexing it.
When Germany defeated and took control of France in 1940, parts of French Africa broke apart to form Free France to fight Germany.
The UK also favored an invasion of Italy from Africa so throughout the war, UK managed to defeat the Italians easily.
Seeing that Italy was struggling in Africa, Germany sent troops to help Italy and the war in Africa turned to a stalemate.
Eventually the US joined the war and helped the UK push the axis out of Africa, and the axis retreated due to the allies supieor numbers.
By 1943, the Allies had completelty pushed the axis out of Africa and began to invade Italy.
